1. Acceptance

By using SafeMe, you agree to use the app responsibly, lawfully, and safely. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

2. What SafeMe is

SafeMe is a personal safety helper. It helps you organize basic safety actions such as SOS messaging, trusted contacts, optional location sharing, and optional account sync.

SafeMe provides support tools. It does not guarantee protection, rescue, monitoring, message delivery, or emergency response.

3. Not an emergency service

SafeMe does not replace police, ambulance, fire services, or any official emergency authority.

In immediate danger, call your local emergency number (for example 112 in many European countries) or contact local authorities directly. SafeMe is not an official emergency response service.

4. Responsible use

You agree to use SafeMe only for lawful and responsible purposes. You must not:

  • Send false or misleading alerts.
  • Harass or abuse trusted contacts.
  • Use location sharing for unlawful tracking of others.
  • Add contacts without appropriate permission or consent.

You are responsible for how you activate, send, or share information through the app.

5. SOS and messaging

SOS may open your device’s SMS app or other tools with a prepared message. You are responsible for checking recipients, message content, and sending.

SafeMe does not guarantee that a message will be sent, delivered, or read. Delivery depends on your device, mobile network, browser, permissions, SMS app, and other technical factors.

6. Location accuracy

Location shown or shared through SafeMe may be inaccurate, delayed, or unavailable. Accuracy can be affected by GPS, permissions, network quality, device settings, battery, and system or browser limits.

Do not rely on SafeMe location alone in urgent situations.

7. Trusted contacts and device availability

You are responsible for keeping trusted contact details accurate and up to date.

You are also responsible for your phone, SMS capability, mobile data or Wi‑Fi connection, and the apps needed to send messages when you choose to use SOS features.

8. Account and sync

Signing in is optional. Basic SOS use can remain available without an account, subject to device and browser support.

When signed in, some data may sync through Supabase or other backend services. Sync depends on internet connectivity and service availability.

9. Service availability

SafeMe does not promise uninterrupted app or sync service. The app may be affected by technical issues, device limits, lack of connectivity, system permissions, or factors outside SafeMe’s control.

10. Changes

SafeMe features, text, settings, and sync behavior may change over time. These terms may also be updated before or after app changes.
